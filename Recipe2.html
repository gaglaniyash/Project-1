Macroni

Macaroni is a type of pasta that is short, tube-shaped, and made from durum wheat, and flour. It can be cooked in a variety of ways, including with butter and cheese, tomato sauce, or baked in the oven with breadcrumbs.

Macroni
Ingredients Quantity
oil 2 tsp
butter 1 tbsp
chopped garlic, clove 2
onion (finely chopped) 1/2
tomato pure 1.5 cup
chilli and pepper powder 1/2 tsp
salt 1/2 tsp
tomato sauce 2 tbsp
water and milk 1/2 cup
processed cheese 1/4 cup
mozzarella cheese 1/2 cup
Preparation time: 30min

Cooking Intructions:

  1. firstly, in a pan heat 2 tsp oil, 1 tbsp butter, 2 clove garlic and ½ tsp chilli flakes
  2. saute until the spices turn aromatic.
  3. add ½ onion and saute until the onion shrinks slightly.
  4. further, add 1½ cup tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.
  5. also add ½ tsp chilli powder, ½ tsp pepper powder, ½ tsp salt and 2 tbsp tomato sauce.
  6. mix well making sure everything is well combined.
  7. add in ½ cup water, ½ cup milk and mix well.
  8. now to cook the pasta, in a large saucepan take water and 1 tsp salt.
  9. once the water comes to a boil, add 1½ cup macaroni pasta.
  10. boil for 8 minutes or until the pasta turns al dente. make sure to check the instruction on the package to know the cooking time.
  11. once the pasta is cooked, drain off the water and transfer to the prepared sauce.
  12. mix well making sure everything is well combined.
  13. add ¼ cup processed cheese and ½ cup mozzarella cheese.
  14. simmer until the cheese melts completely.
  15. finally, mix well and enjoy Macaroni and Cheese recipe when it is piping hot.
